## QUANTUM COMPUTING ROADMAP

| Milestone | Target Date | Status |
|-----------|-------------|--------|
| **Cepheus-1-108Q (108-qubit system)** | April 2026 | ✅ **GA Achieved** (99.1% 2Q fidelity @ 60ns) |
| Cepheus-1-108Q → 99.5% fidelity | End of 2026 | 🎯 In progress |
| **150+ qubit system (99.7% fidelity)** | End of 2026 | 🎯 Targeted |
| **1,000+ qubit system (99.8% fidelity)** | End of 2027 | 🎯 Targeted |
| **UK 1,000+ qubit system** | 3–4 years (2029–2030) | 🎯 Announced |
| **Quantum Advantage** | ~3 years (2029) | CEO estimate (updated roadmap expected H2 2026) |

**Chiplet Architecture:** Rigetti uses proprietary multi-chip tiling — Cepheus-1-108Q uses 12 interconnected 9-qubit chiplets, the industry's largest modular quantum system.

**Gate Performance:**
- Prototype: 99.9% 2-qubit fidelity at 28ns (adiabatic CZ gate)
- 9-qubit system: 99.8% median 2-qubit fidelity at 40ns
- 36-qubit system: 99.6% 2-qubit fidelity
- 108-qubit system: 99.1% 2-qubit fidelity at ~60ns (targeting 99.5%)
- All systems: 99.9% single-qubit fidelity
- Gate speeds: 50–70ns (~1,000× faster than trapped-ion/neutral-atom)

## RECENT KEY DEVELOPMENTS (2026)

### 1. 108-Qubit System General Availability (April 7, 2026)
Cepheus-1-108Q — the industry's largest modular quantum computer — is now generally available via Rigetti QCS, **Amazon Braket**, **Microsoft Azure Quantum**, and **qBraid**. Comprises 12 interconnected 9-qubit chiplets.
- [Press Release](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/04/07/3269628/0/en/Rigetti-Announces-General-Availability-of-108-Qubit-System.html)

### 2. U.S. Government CHIPS Act Deal (May 21, 2026)
Signed a Letter of Intent with the **U.S. Department of Commerce** for up to **$100 million** in CHIPS Act funding over 3 years to accelerate superconducting quantum computing R&D. The government would receive an equity stake.
- [Press Release](https://investors.rigetti.com/news-releases/news-release-details/rigetti-signs-letter-intent-us-government-quantum-computing)

### 3. India C-DAC $8.4M Order (January 20, 2026)
Received an **$8.4 million purchase order** from India's Centre for Development of Advanced Computing (C-DAC) for a 108-qubit on-premises quantum computer, to be deployed at Bengaluru in H2 2026.
- [Press Release](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/01/20/3221654/0/en/Rigetti-Announces-Order-for-a-108-Qubit-Quantum-Computer-from-India-s-Centre-for-Development-of-Advanced-Computing-C-DAC.html)

### 4. UK $100M Investment (March 25, 2026)
Announced intention to invest up to **$100 million** in the UK to deploy a 1,000+ qubit quantum computing system within 3–4 years — the company's first major investment outside the US.
- [Press Release](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/03/25/3262574/0/en/Rigetti-Computing-Intends-to-Invest-100-Million-in-UK-to-Accelerate-Quantum-Computing-Development.html)

### 5. Q1 2026 Earnings (May 11, 2026)
Revenue of $4.4M (+132% YoY from ~$1.9M in Q1 2025). Operating loss $26.0M. Strong cash position of $569M with no debt. Revenue growth driven by Novera QPU shipments and cloud access.
- [Press Release](https://investors.rigetti.com/news-releases/news-release-details/rigetti-computing-reports-first-quarter-2026-financial-results)

### 6. Amazon Braket Launch (June 1, 2026)
Amazon Braket launched Cepheus-1-108Q as a generally available device — the first 100+ qubit gate-based device on Braket.
- [AWS Blog](https://aws.amazon.com/blogs/quantum-computing/amazon-braket-launches-rigetti-cepheus-1-108q-superconducting-device/)

### 7. Error Mitigation Breakthrough (May 27, 2026)
Error mitigation on Ankaa-3 produced scientifically meaningful results in plasma physics simulation, recovering wave-propagation data otherwise obscured by hardware noise.
- [Medium Blog](https://medium.com/@rigetticomputing/error-mitigation-unlocks-quantum-simulation-of-plasma-waves-babb23ecbf0d)

### 8. University of Saskatchewan Novera QPU Sale (March 31, 2026)
Sold a 9-qubit Novera QPU to the University of Saskatchewan — the core of their first quantum computing system.
- [Press Release](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/03/31/3266005/0/en/Rigetti-Announces-Novera-QPU-Sale-to-the-University-of-Saskatchewan.html)

## KEY COMPETITIVE POSITIONING

- **Only public pure-play quantum computing company** with vertically integrated full-stack approach (chip design → fabrication at Fab-1 → cloud delivery)
- **Proprietary chiplet-based architecture** — industry-first multi-chip processor, path to scaling beyond monolithic limits
- **Fastest gate speeds** among major modalities: 50–70ns vs. trapped-ion/neutral-atom (~1,000× slower)
- **Strong balance sheet**: ~$569M cash, zero debt as of Q1 2026
- **Revenue growing rapidly** but from a small base: $7.1M (FY2025) → $4.4M (Q1 2026 alone)
- **Highly shorted**: 18.77% of float (but short ratio only 1.24 days given massive daily volume ~40M shares)

## SUMMARY

Rigetti Computing (RGTI) is a pre-revenue-scale quantum computing company with a $7.1B market cap, trading at $21.36 with a YTD loss of -9.49%. The company achieved a major milestone in April 2026 with the general availability of its 108-qubit Cepheus-1-108Q system — the industry's largest modular quantum computer — now accessible through Amazon Braket, Microsoft Azure, and its own QCS platform. Q1 2026 revenue reached $4.4M (+132% YoY), though the company still operates at a significant non-GAAP loss of $14.7M. Rigetti's $569M cash position (zero debt) plus a pending $100M CHIPS Act LOI with the U.S. Department of Commerce provide substantial runway. The technology roadmap targets a 150+ qubit system by end of 2026, 1,000+ qubits by end of 2027, and quantum advantage within ~3 years. Key risks include execution on aggressive roadmap targets, heavy short interest (18.77%), and the need to continue improving fidelity while scaling. Recent wins include an $8.4M C-DAC order (India), a $100M UK investment commitment, and growing adoption across government, academic, and commercial customers.
