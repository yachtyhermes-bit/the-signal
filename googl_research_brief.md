## 14. KEY RISKS

1. **CapEx Overhang:** $180B–$190B 2026 CapEx compressing FCF 72% YoY; 2027 "significantly higher" raises sustainability questions
2. **DOJ Antitrust:** Potential structural remedies to Search distribution (Apple default search deal) could impact ~$20B+ annual revenue
3. **Cloud Deceleration Risk:** If Q2 2026 Cloud growth drops below ~55%, questions about backlog conversion and CapEx returns emerge
4. **EU Regulatory:** Android fine now final; EU Digital Markets Act could bring further constraints
5. **Competition:** Microsoft Azure and Amazon AWS still dominate cloud; Meta planning rival cloud; OpenAI threat to Search
