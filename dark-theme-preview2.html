SK Hynix CEO Warns 2027 Will Be Worst Memory Shortage in History

By The Signal — July 16, 2026 · 5 min read

SK Hynix CEO Kwak Noh-jung told Reuters Friday that 2027 will bring the most severe memory supply crunch the industry has ever seen, and demand will keep running ahead of capacity through 2030 — just days after the company's record-breaking $26.5B Nasdaq IPO.

"We forecast that next year will be the worst year in the industry's history from the supply perspective," Kwak said. "Our customer demand continues to go up, while our capacity has limitations."
