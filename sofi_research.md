## 7. Key Takeaways

1. **Strong Revenue Growth:** SoFi grew revenue 42.6% YoY in Q1 2026 to $1.10B, with TTM revenue of $3.94B. Full-year 2026 guidance calls for ~$4.66B (+30%).

2. **Profitable at Scale:** GAAP net income of $167M in Q1 2026 (+134% YoY), 10th consecutive profitable quarter. Profit margins expanding (15% net margin in Q1).

3. **Record Loan Originations:** $12.2B in Q1 (+68% YoY), driven by strength across personal ($8.3B), student ($2.6B, +119%), and home loans ($1.2B, +137%).

4. **Member Growth:** 14.7M members (+35% YoY), adding 1.05M in Q1 alone. Cross-buy rate of 43% showing strong platform engagement.

5. **Stock Down 33.6% YTD** despite record operating results — primarily due to management maintaining (not raising) full-year guidance amid "no rate cuts in 2026" assumption, and Tech Platform weakness.

6. **Tech Platform is the Weak Spot:** Revenue declined 27% YoY to $75M from losing a major client. Full-year guidance of ~$325M requires a dramatic H2 turnaround.

7. **Analyst Consensus is Hold** with wide dispersion ($12–$31 targets). Recent price target cuts by most major firms post-Q1 earnings, though upside to consensus target ($19–$23) is ~15–25%.
