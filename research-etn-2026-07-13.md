## 3. The Story Angle

Eaton is the structural bottleneck in the AI data center buildout -- the only company that covers the full power chain from utility substation to GPU chip-level liquid cooling. After +23% YTD, the stock has pulled back ~8% from its 52-week high (36.74), creating a window ahead of Q2 earnings on July 31.
