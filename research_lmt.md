## HYPERSONIC PROGRAMS

### Dark Eagle (LRHW – Long-Range Hypersonic Weapon)
**Source:** [Overt Defense – $2.7B Contract](https://www.overtdefense.com/2026/04/27/us-army-awards-2-7-billion-dark-eagle-hypersonic-weapon-contract/)  
**Source:** [Army Recognition – Deployment Timeline](https://www.armyrecognition.com/news/army-news/2026/us-army-to-deploy-first-operational-dark-eagle-hypersonic-missile-with-3-500-km-range-in-coming-weeks)  
**Source:** [CRS Report – Dark Eagle](https://news.usni.org/2026/04/09/report-to-congress-on-u-s-armys-dark-eagle-hypersonic-weapon)

- **Program:** U.S. Army's first ground-launched, mobile hypersonic strike system
- **$2.7 billion** multiyear contract awarded late March 2026 (Army Contracting Command-Redstone)
- Combines R&D, testing, and production under single contract
- Lockheed Martin role: Two-stage solid rocket booster (with Northrop Grumman, Aerojet)
- Common Hypersonic Glide Body (C-HGB): Built by Dynetics (joint Army-Navy design)
- Range: ~1,725 miles (~2,775 km)
- **First operational battery deployment expected 2026** (Lt. Gen. Lozano, March 18, 2026)
- Production rate: Currently ~1/month, ramping to 24/year
- Two additional batteries planned by FY2027

### Navy CPS (Conventional Prompt Strike)
- Navy variant of same All-Up Round missile
- $1.36B Navy contract modification to Lockheed (April 2026) for common hypersonic missile work
- Deployment: Zumwalt-class destroyers, Virginia-class submarines
- Source: [Overt Defense](https://www.overtdefense.com/2026/04/27/us-army-awards-2-7-billion-dark-eagle-hypersonic-weapon-contract/)

## KEY TAKEAWAYS

1. **Revenue Growth:** LMT has grown revenue from $67.6B (FY2023) → $71.0B (FY2024) → $75.0B (FY2025), with guidance for ~$78B in FY2026 (~5% growth).

2. **F-35 Is the Backbone:** Record 191 deliveries in 2025, $24.3B Lots 18-19 deal, ~416-jet backlog. Q1 2026 deliveries slowed (32 vs 47) but sustainment contracts remain strong ($2.29B in June).

3. **Missiles & Fire Control Is the Growth Engine:** +8% YoY in Q1 2026. Driven by PAC-3, JASSM/LRASM, PrSM, and THAAD. Multi-year framework agreements signed with DoD to 3-4x production rates.

4. **Hypersonics Are Here:** Dark Eagle deployment imminent (2026). $2.7B LRHW contract + $1.36B Navy CPS modification. Lockheed builds the booster; production ramping to 24/year.

5. **Cash Flow Recovery Expected:** Q1 2026 FCF was -$291M (working capital timing), but full-year guidance is $6.5-6.8B — implying a massive H2 2026 ramp.

6. **Low Beta Defensive Stock:** Beta of 0.106. YTD +4.01%. Trailing P/E 24.76x, forward P/E 15.92x suggests significant earnings growth is priced in.
