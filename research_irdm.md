## Major Catalysts

### 1. Aireon Acquisition (May 14, 2026)
- **Iridium agreed to acquire the remaining 61% of Aireon Holdings** for full ownership.
- Aireon operates the world's only space-based ADS-B air traffic surveillance system, hosted on Iridium's satellite constellation.
- Customer partnerships extended through at least **2035** as part of the transaction.
- Source: [Yahoo Finance](https://finance.yahoo.com/markets/stocks/articles/iridium-takes-full-control-aireon-101449402.html)

### 2. DoD / U.S. Space Force — EMSS Contract
- **Seven-year, $738.5 million** fixed-price airtime contract with U.S. Space Force, signed **September 2019**.
- Iridium provides unlimited satellite airtime for DoD personnel worldwide.
- Fixed-price rate increased to **$110.5 million/year** for the contract year beginning **September 15, 2025**.
- Government service revenue grew **3% YoY** to $27.6M in Q1 2026.
