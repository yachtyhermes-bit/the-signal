## Summary Assessment
Netflix is in a strong fundamental position (16% revenue growth, 86% earnings growth, 48% ROE, $13.4B net income) but faces near-term headwinds: the stock is down ~40% from highs, Q2 guidance disappointed, and Reed Hastings' board exit shook confidence.
