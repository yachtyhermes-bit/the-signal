Methodology

How We Invest

Every position in the Signal portfolio is a high-conviction, long-term bet on a business we'd own for 5–10 years. We start with quality — strong balance sheets, proven competitive moats, and companies generating real free cash flow at reasonable valuations. Then we wait for the chart to give us a favorable entry. No chasing. No FOMO. Just signal.

Discipline

Conviction Over Timing

We don’t use stop losses. Volatility is the price of admission, not a reason to exit. Risk is managed through position sizing and selectivity — we’d rather own 25 companies we deeply understand than 100 we barely know. No single position breaks 10%, ensuring no one bet takes down the portfolio. We buy thesis, not tickers.
