## COMPETITIVE MOAT ANALYSIS

1. **High Switching Costs** — Once a police department adopts Axon Evidence/Cloud, migrating years of bodycam footage is operationally prohibitive. Multi-year contracts create sticky recurring revenue.
2. **Network Effects / Data Flywheel** — Axon's AI (Draft One, Assistant) improves with more data from its installed base of 17,000+ agencies. Competitors can't replicate the dataset.
3. **Regulatory & Procurement Moat** — Public safety procurement cycles are slow; Axon has deep relationships with federal, state, and local governments worldwide.
4. **Integrated Ecosystem** — TASER + Body Camera + Cloud + AI creates a unified platform. Competitors (Motorola, WatchGuard) offer pieces but not the full stack.
5. **Brand / Market Dominance** — TASER is synonymous with conducted energy weapons. Axon dominates the U.S. body camera market (~80%+ share).
6. **Counter-Drone Pivot** — Expanding into drone security opens adjacent TAM (Pentagon FY2027 budget: $54B for autonomous systems).
