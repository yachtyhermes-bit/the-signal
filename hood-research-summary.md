## KEY STORY ANGLE

> **"From Meme-Stock Punchline to $90B Fintech Juggernaut: Robinhood's Unlikely Transformation"**

Robinhood has completed a stunning financial turnaround. The company that lost over $1B in 2022 and symbolized the meme-stock frenzy is now printing $1.88B in annual profit with 92% gross margins and nearly $5B in revenue — growing 50%+ annually for three straight years. The stock trades at 48x earnings and is down 35% from its 52-week high, presenting a valuation debate: is HOOD an expensive growth story or a misunderstood fintech powerhouse? With new revenue streams in prediction markets, credit cards, AI agents, and crypto, Robinhood is expanding far beyond its origins. The Q2 2026 earnings report on July 29 will be a critical test — analysts expect $1.265B in revenue and $0.42 EPS, and any weakness could exacerbate the recent pullback.
