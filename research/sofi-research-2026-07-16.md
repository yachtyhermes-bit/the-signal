### 11. UNIQUE ANGLE
SoFi started as a student loan refi company. Today it's a nationally chartered bank with 14.7M members, a USD stablecoin (first by a US national bank), AI-powered investing, small business loans, and the #1 ranked DIY investor platform. The national bank charter is the moat most fintechs can't copy — they can issue stablecoins, hold deposits, and offer banking products other fintechs can't touch. Q2 earnings July 29 will test if the market re-rates this story.
