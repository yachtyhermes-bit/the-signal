## 7. COMPETITIVE MOAT SUMMARY

### Strengths
1. **AWS scale**: $150B run rate, 38 regions, 120 AZs — unmatched global cloud footprint
2. **Custom silicon**: Trainium + Graviton create 30-40% cost advantage vs GPU/x86, >$20B run rate
3. **AI platform lock-in**: Bedrock is sticky — 170% QoQ spend growth, multi-year contracts
4. **Retail margin expansion**: International turned profitable; North America margins expanding
5. **$364B backlog**: 2.4 years of revenue coverage provides exceptional visibility
6. **Anthropic relationship**: $100B+ commitment, exclusive partnership value

### Risks
1. **$200B capex plan**: Massive investment with 18-36 month monetization lag
2. **Cloud share erosion**: 33% → 28% market share since 2021
3. **Azure competition**: Growing faster (40% vs 28%) with enterprise bundling advantage
4. **Kuiper/Leo execution risk**: 4 years behind Starlink, deadline challenges
5. **Free cash flow compression**: From $32B to $7.7B as capex surges
6. **Margin pressure on inference economics**: No hyperscaler proven positive ROI at AI scale
