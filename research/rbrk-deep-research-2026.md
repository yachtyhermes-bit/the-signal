## 5. STRUCTURAL THESIS — Why This Is Secular

### Ransomware Is Board-Level Now
Every major breach — Colonial Pipeline, Change Healthcare, MGM, CDK — has turned data resilience from an IT checkbox into a board-level imperative. Rubrik's immutable, append-only architecture means attackers can't encrypt or delete backups. That's becoming non-negotiable.

### Data Sovereignty Is a Growth Engine
The EU, UK, India, and others are tightening data residency requirements. Rubrik's AWS European Sovereign Cloud play directly addresses this. The $500M UK investment signals serious commitment to international markets.

### Cloud Migration Creates Complexity
Enterprises are running hybrid workloads across on-prem, multi-cloud (AWS, Azure, GCP), and SaaS (M365, Google Workspace, Salesforce). Each new surface area needs protection. Rubrik's unified platform is built for this — competitors are still stitching together point solutions.

### AI Agents Are the Next Attack Surface
Rubrik is early on securing AI agents (Amazon Bedrock, Google Cloud, Anthropic). As enterprises deploy AI agents at scale, the data security requirements explode. This is a greenfield TAM expansion.

### The "Backup" Category Is Getting Replaced
Gartner now calls it "Enterprise Backup and Recovery" — but the next Magic Quadrant will likely be "Cyber Resilience Platforms." Rubrik is defining the category. Commvault, Veeam, and Cohesity are playing catch-up on messaging.

### Subscription Model Is Locking In Customers
Subscription ARR is the real story — ~$1.57B and growing 39%+. Customers start with backup, expand into threat monitoring, identity resilience, AI governance. Land-and-expand at scale.

Key themes from recent appearances:
