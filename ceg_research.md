## KEY DEALS & CATALYSTS (Verified with Sources)

### 1. Microsoft / Three Mile Island "Crane Clean Energy Center" Restart

- **Date:** September 2024 (contract signed)
- **Power:** 835 MW of new baseload nuclear power
- **Location:** Three Mile Island Unit 1, now renamed "Crane Clean Energy Center" (Pennsylvania)
- **Status:** Restart on track for 2027
- **DOE Loan:** $1 billion federal loan guarantee (announced November 2025)
- **REGULATORY:** FERC granted waiver June 2026; NRC approval close
- **Significance:** First restart of a previously retired nuclear plant in U.S. history; deal kept secret until announcement

### 3. Walmart / Dresden Clean Energy Center Deal

- **Date:** June 23, 2026 (just announced)
- **Terms:** 15-year PPA for 176 MW of nuclear power (including 30 MW of expanded capacity via uprates)
- **Location:** Dresden Clean Energy Center, Illinois
- **Delivery:** Two 15-year terms beginning 2029 and 2030
- **Significance:** Walmart's first-ever nuclear PPA; one of the first between a large retailer and a nuclear facility in the U.S.
- **License:** Dresden licensed to operate through 2049 and 2051
- **Jobs:** 1,100+ family-sustaining jobs at Dresden
- **Quote:** Jim McHugh (Constellation SVP): "Walmart's commitment enables meaningful investment in the Dresden Clean Energy Center"

**Source:** Constellation Energy press release (June 23, 2026): https://www.constellationenergy.com/newsroom/2026/06/constellation-and-walmart-announce-long-term-agreement-to-support-reliable-emissions-free-nuclear-energy-in-illinois.html

6. **Uprates:** Efficiency upgrades increasing output without new builds (e.g., 30 MW via Walmart deal).

## KEY ANGLES FOR ARTICLE

### Angle 1: "The Nuclear Arms Race for AI Power"
Microsoft (TMI/835MW), Meta (Clinton/20yr), Walmart (Dresden/176MW), CyrusOne — every major tech company is now locking in nuclear baseload for AI compute. Constellation is the single biggest supplier.

### Angle 2: "From $412 to $265: Is the Market Wrong About Nuclear?"
Despite $94.7B market cap, 55 GW capacity, and the best contract pipeline in nuclear history, the stock has dropped 25.9% YTD. Forward P/E 19.6x. Target $360+. 63.8% revenue growth. Something is pricing in risk — but analysts see 36% upside.

### Angle 3: "The Government Is Betting $1 Billion on Three Mile Island"
First nuclear restart in U.S. history. DOE loan, FERC waiver, Trump administration political capital — all backing Constellation's Crane Clean Energy Center. If this works, it's a template for retiring plants across America. If it fails, billions are at stake.
