## RECENT NEWS

### 1. New AI Networking Platform Launch — 7060XE7 Series / 1.6T Platform (June 9, 2026)

Arista Networks launched its new **1.6T networking platform series** targeting large-scale AI infrastructure. The launch centers on the **7060XE7 Series**, purpose-built for AI data center fabrics.

**Key details:**
- **7060XE7 Series:** AI-centric switch platform with 1.6T Ethernet capacity
- Part of the broader **R4 family expansion** (following Oct 2025 additions of 7020, 7280, and 7800 models)
- Built on Arista's EOS (Extensible Operating System) software stack
- Targeted at AI cluster interconnects for hyperscalers

## KEY TAKEAWAYS

1. **AI infrastructure play is real and growing:** ANET delivered $9.71B TTM revenue at 35.1% growth with 38.3% net margins. Q1 2026 revenue hit $2.71B (up 35.1% YoY).

2. **1.6T platform launch (Jun 9) validates AI networking thesis** — Arista is moving aggressively to capture hyperscaler AI cluster demand, competing with Broadcom and NVIDIA in the AI fabric layer.

3. **Bechtolsheim's $73.88M insider sale (460K shares)** is notable but context matters: he retains 182.8M shares (~$28.6B worth). The sale represents ~0.25% of his holdings. Option exercises at $14-15 strikes (vs $158-167 sale price) suggest profit-taking on deeply in-the-money options rather than a negative signal.

4. **Valuation pullback (-13% from highs)** creates an entry debate: 53.75x trailing P/E is premium, but 35.15x forward P/E with 35%+ growth may be justified. Analysts remain overwhelmingly bullish with a median $187 target.

5. **Supply constraints** flagged by multiple analysts (Trefis, Yahoo Finance) as the key risk — can Arista secure enough components to meet surging AI demand?

6. **Wall Street consensus is unanimous bullish:** 27 analysts, zero Holds or Sells, median target $187 (19.6% upside). MarketBeat notes price target raised to $200. Seeking Alpha calls ANET "the quiet winner of the AI arms race."
