## 2. AGENTFORCE — AI AGENT PLATFORM

### What It Is
- Enterprise-grade platform for building, deploying, and managing autonomous AI agents
- Agents handle sales, service, marketing, and commerce workflows autonomously
- Touted as "digital labor revolution" by CEO Marc Benioff
- Embeds AI agents directly into Salesforce's CRM data layer (the moat)
- Processes 28.6 trillion tokens per quarter, 3.8 billion agentic work units

### Key Metrics (as of Q1 FY2027)
- **$1B+ ARR** in under 12 months — *fastest product ramp in company history*
- **9,500+ paid deals**
- **Token usage up 152% QoQ**
- Rebranded all cloud products under Agentforce umbrella
- 3+ pricing models (per-workload, per-conversation, seat-based)

### Adoption Evidence
- PenFed Credit Union: 76 AI agents deployed, 10% call-handle time reduction, 50% after-call work reduction, ~$1.6M/year savings
- UCLA Health: first customer-facing Agentforce deployment in 8 months
- Adecco: expanded deal targeting majority AI-driven revenue
- Commerce release: "biggest Agentforce Commerce release yet" (June 24, 2026)

### Bearish Counterpoint (Recent)
- **KeyBanc (July 15-17):** Questions Agentforce product maturity — cites weak customer traction
- **The Register (July 15):** "Agentforce isn't winning over clients"
- Morgan Stanley slashed price target by 35% (July 22)
